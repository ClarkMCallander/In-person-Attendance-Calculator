Remote Day Allowance Calculator

Understanding the Rules:

  • Each team member is allowed 1 remote day per 5-day work week.
  • Of the remaining 4 in-person days, you need to be in-person at least 72% of the time.
  • This means a maximum of 28% of those in-person days can be "missed" (not counting your weekly remote day).
  • Sick days, holidays, and vacation days contribute to this 28% missable allowance.

Your Information:

Default company holidays are based on typical Ford US salaried employee schedules, including major holidays and a Christmas/New Year's shutdown period. Please adjust if your specific holiday count differs.
